Is This Right for You?

Who the Express Reset Is For

✓ Good fit if your car is...

  • Generally clean, just dusty or lightly dirty
  • A daily driver that needs regular upkeep
  • Recently detailed and needs a refresh
  • Needed clean before a specific event or trip

Consider upgrading if your car has...

  • Interior stains or heavy pet hair
  • Odors that need deeper treatment
  • Paint contamination (rough or gritty feel)
  • Not been properly detailed in a long time

Not sure which package is right for you? Reach out and we'll recommend the right one for your vehicle's condition.

Questions

Express Reset FAQ

Most Express Resets take 1 to 1.5 hours. Larger vehicles (trucks, SUVs) may take slightly longer. We give you a more specific estimate when you book.

The Express Reset covers the exterior thoroughly plus a quick interior vacuum. The Essential Reset adds a full interior clean — deep vacuuming, seat shampoo or wipe-down, dashboard and trim detail, odor treatment, and interior glass. If your interior needs real attention, the Essential is the right choice.

The standard Express Reset includes a hand wash and dry without a wax coat. If you'd like a spray wax or quick detailer applied for short-term protection, ask us when you book — we can often include it as an add-on for a small additional cost.

For most daily drivers, every 2–4 weeks works well to maintain a clean appearance. Pairing regular Express Resets with a deeper Essential Reset every few months is a great maintenance routine.
